# Privacy Notice

**Last Updated:** November 8, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Falbor**, an AI platform that helps users create websites automatically. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect information while using Falbor.  

**Age Guidance:** Falbor is intended for users **10 years and older**. Younger children should use the platform under parental supervision.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### User Inputs
- Prompts and messages submitted to the AI  
- Generated website content and project files  
- Account details (email, username)

### Technical Data
- IP address, device, and browser type  
- Usage logs and session data  
- Cookies for session management

### Optional Data
- GitHub links or project repositories for integration

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:  
- Provide and improve AI website generation  
- Store user prompts, AI responses, and project files securely  
- Communicate updates or changes to the platform  
- Ensure service integrity and security

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All prompts, AI outputs, and projects are stored on secure servers  
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest  
- Access is limited to authorized personnel only  

---

## 5. AI Projects and Free Credits

- Users can submit prompts to generate websites  
- Free credits refresh periodically  
- Project data and AI responses are stored for your account only  

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

- Clerk is used for authentication  
- Hosting and database providers support service functionality  
- Optional analytics may be used to improve Falbor  

---

## 7. User Rights

You can:  
- View your submitted prompts and generated websites  
- Delete your account and associated content at any time  
- Export your projects if needed  

Contact: **contact@falbor.ai**  

---

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

- Used for sessions, preferences, and service improvement  
- Do **not** track personal content of AI submissions  

---

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Notice

- Updates may occur as the platform evolves  
- Important changes will be posted on this page  
- Users should review this notice periodically
